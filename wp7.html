1 Work package number: WP7
2 Work package title: Verification-validation of methodology, dissemination and exploitation
3 Starting Month: 23
4 Ending Month: 34
5 Person Months (PMs): 12
6 Objectives: In WP7, the components developed in WPs 2 to 6 will be integrated under a BIM protocol.
7 Description: Objectives: The objective is to perform small-scale demonstration in order to validate the components. Description of Work: The numerical results obtained in WP4 will be compared to corresponding experimental results of WP6 as a means of validating the overall optimization / design / manufacturing methodology. The optimization algorithms developed in WP2 will then be employed for the design of a wider range of case studies at real scale, which will be used for further demonstrating the methodology and disseminating the research results. In WP7 verification and validation procedures will be implemented for each of the software components of the proposed project. An extensive survey of the existing scientific literature and technological standards will be conducted. Verification and validation methods will apply, where applicable, existing acknowledged methodologies. Task 7.1 Verification: This task involves the process of ensuring that the conceptual model has been carefully transformed into a computer model with sufficient accuracy; in other words, the mathematical concern of building the model right will be addressed. Early verification tests will be applied during the development of each module in order to correct unexpected deviations. Task 7.2 Validation: This task involves the process of ensuring that the conceptual model is sufficiently accurate to represent the real world from the perspective of its intended uses; in other words, the physical concern of building the right model will be addressed. It will be applied only after the verification has been done. Standard experiment outcomes will be considered. Task 7.3 Dissemination: The scientific and technical outcomes of the project will be published in the best journals in the field of structural engineering and design optimization, as well as in journals specialized in additive manufacturing. It is also planned to present the developed methods and achieved results in selected international conferences and workshops in those fields as well as to specialized exhibitions, summits and showcases.

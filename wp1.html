1 Work package number: WP1
2 Work package title: Project management and coordination
3 Starting Month: 1
4 Ending Month: 36
5 Description: Objectives: In WP1 the administrative and financial obligations of the team members will be performed, including coordination of the project, reporting, supervision and communication support. Description of work: PI will supervise the financial status of the project, negotiate contractual and administrative issues or possible project modifications, revise project planning when appropriate, allocate project resources and schedule tasks in case that modifications turn out to be necessary or advantageous. Primarily PI aims at preventing any deviation from the original work plan (e.g. by ensuring that results from the WPs are available on time and are of high quality). PI ensures that WPs are supported adequately in his responsibility for administrative coordination and performance control of the Task-related operations, (e.g. coststatements, summary reports, deliverables etc.) ensuring that adequate resources are at the disposal of the project. Regarding the financial progress, PI will follow-up project expenses and track deviations. PI will perform cost management activity which includes the processes required to ensure that the project is completed within the approved budget. Concerning cost control, PI will abide project requirements, including monitoring of the cost performance to detect deviations from the original plan, accurate recording in the cost baseline, preventing of incorrect, inappropriate, or un-authorized changes of the contract. All required reporting documents to GGET/ESPA will be created within WP1 by PI. In addition, the relevant research activities performed at European and international level in the field of the project and related topics will be closely monitored by PI throughout the duration of the project. Contacts with the organisations performing such research and/or industrial activities will be established through PI aiming to avoid work duplication, to maximise project efficiency and to activate synergies between projects.

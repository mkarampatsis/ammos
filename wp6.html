1 Work package number: WP6
2 Work package title: Experimental testing of the 3D printed specimens
3 Starting Month: 19
4 Ending Month: 33
5 Description: Objectives: In order to justify that the structural capacity of the 3D printed specimens is in compliance with the one predicted numerically; experimental testing will be performed. Description of Work: Experimental tests of the 3D printed specimens manufactured in WP5 will be carried out. Tests will be displacement-controlled, based on the numerical simulations of WP4. Specimens will be loaded to collapse in order to determine their ultimate strength. Tests will be performed at the Institute of Steel Structures at NTUA, using the available infrastructure, including strong floor, testing frames, actuators and measuring devices, properly connected to a centralized control system. As stated in WP5 for purposes of verification of the results obtained, three specimens will be tested for each one of the following three categories: (a) one structural element, (b) one joint and (c) one simple structure.
